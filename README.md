# Ring Hyacinth / 海辛

AI filmmaker, director, and technical artist based in Shanghai.

I create AI-native animated films and build the small-studio systems behind them: storyboards, prompt languages, local-first production tools, agent workflows, and model research.

My favorite creative shape is simple: a tiny action opens a world. Cats walk into museums, cities, artworks, and public screens, carrying the story with lightness while the production pipeline stays rigorous.

## Selected Work

- **《故宫猫猫上班记》 / “Cats Working In The Forbidden City”** - created with Simon Lee. Featured by [TED2025](https://blog.ted.com/unlimited-imagination-the-epic-interstitials-of-ted2025/) and shown as the opening film for Session 9, [“Inside Out”](https://tedlive.ted.com/webcasts/ted2025/session/836). The original film was published through [Beijing Daily](https://xinwen.bjd.com.cn/content/s678a4697e4b08edd28f3f16c.html).
- **《文明小猫游浦东》 / “Civilization Kitten Travels Pudong”** - a city-culture AI film that brings Pudong landmarks into a warm, public-screen friendly cat narrative. Public coverage: [Xinhua / Xinmin Weekly](https://sh.news.cn/20251225/469df4d171bd4e58937b0f41eb4e2865/c.html).
- **《来自卢浮宫的猫》 / “A Cat from the Louvre”** - invited by the Museum of Art Pudong for a Louvre exhibition campaign, using cats, mirrors, Shanghai, Paris, and exhibition objects as one story system. Creative write-up: [QQ News](https://view.inews.qq.com/a/20260116A01VD200).

## What I Build Here

- Local-first creative systems for AI filmmaking.
- Director-style previs, storyboard, and shot-planning workflows.
- Prompt formats that preserve camera, motion, material, rhythm, and causality.
- Lightweight tools for tiny teams making ambitious AI films.
- Experiments at the edge of film, software, and agentic creative work.

## Background

I run **海辛阿文工作室 / Haixin & Awen Studio** with Simon, a tiny AI animation studio exploring warm, cinematic AI stories and practical production workflows.

I have worked as Midjourney CN Content Lead, Morph Studio Product Lead, and Luma AI Technical Artist. My training crosses film and public health: MFA in Film Production from Loyola Marymount University, and Public Health at West China School of Medicine, Sichuan University.
